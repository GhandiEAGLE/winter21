About Procrastination


When the going gets tough, the tough get going. When the lazy get going, the going gets tough. We at Procrastination attempt to stave off both unfortunate outcomes by altogether ignoring the issue. How can a going get tough if the going isn't going? With that in mind, a company philosophy was born, founded upon the principles of noncommittment and disinterest.


We proudly offer very little in the way of actual, physical services. Rather than a tangible product, our results can be seen in the sheer lack of work you've managed to accomplish. Our services will enable you to completely ignore whatever workload you've managed to accrue by working "hard", thus ensuring that you get that glorious rush when last-minute panic sets in.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is simple; help you to not help yourself.


VISION: Our vision is a world where the greatests advancements in efficiency are for the greatest attempts at glorious inefficiency.
